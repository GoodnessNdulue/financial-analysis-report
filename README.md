# Financial Performance Analysis Dashboard

## üìå Project Overview

Built an interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing **$118.73M in sales**, **$16.89M in profit**, and **1.13M units sold** across **5 market segments** and **multiple countries**.

**Goal:** Provide executive leadership with real-time visibility into sales performance, profitability trends, and market segment analysis to drive strategic business decisions.

---

## üìä Key Metrics Tracked

- **Total Sales:** $118.73M
- **Total Profit:** $16.89M
- **Units Sold:** 1.13M
- **Cost of Goods Sold (COGS):** $101.83M
- **Profit Margin:** 14.2%
- **Market Segments:** 5 (Government, Midmarket, Enterprise, Channel Partners, Small Business)
- **Geographic Markets:** 5 countries analyzed

---

## üîç Key Insights

### 1. Sales Performance by Market Segment
- **Government segment** drives **$53M in sales** (44.6% of total revenue)
- **Midmarket** contributes **$42M** (35.4% of revenue)
- **Small Business and Channel Partners** combined represent only **$4M** (3.4%)
- **Critical finding:** Top 2 segments account for **80% of total sales**

### 2. Profitability Analysis by Country
- **Top-performing country:** $3.8M in profit
- **Runner-up markets:** $3.7M, $3.5M in profit
- **Profit distribution** is relatively balanced across top 3 countries (no single market dominance)
- **Opportunity:** Identify what's working in top markets and replicate in underperforming regions

### 3. Monthly Profit Trends - CRITICAL ALERT
- **Peak profit:** $3.4M (early in tracking period)
- **Current profit:** $0.7M (end of tracking period)
- **Decline:** **79% profit drop** from peak to current month
- **üö® RED FLAG:** Sharp, consistent downward trend requires immediate investigation

### 4. Units Sold Distribution
- **Midmarket segment** leads unit volume at **41.81% of total units**
- **Government** follows with **15.29%** despite higher revenue (higher price per unit)
- **Enterprise** accounts for **14.97%** of units
- **Insight:** Midmarket is a volume play, Government is a value play

---

## üí° Recommendations Delivered

### 1. **URGENT: Investigate Profit Decline**
**Priority: Immediate**
- Profit has dropped **79% from peak ($3.4M) to current ($0.7M)**
- **Potential causes to investigate:**
  - Pricing strategy changes (discounts, promotions)
  - Rising COGS (supplier costs, production inefficiencies)
  - Product mix shift (selling more low-margin products)
  - Seasonal effects (if applicable)
  - Competitive pressure forcing price reductions
- **Action:** Conduct root cause analysis within 7 days
- **Escalate:** Present findings to executive team for strategic intervention

### 2. **Diversify Revenue Concentration**
- **80% of sales** come from just 2 segments (Government + Midmarket)
- **Risk:** High exposure if either segment faces disruption
- **Recommendation:**
  - Develop growth strategy for Enterprise segment (currently only 14.97% of units)
  - Invest in Channel Partners (currently only 14.32% of units)
  - Create targeted campaigns for Small Business (currently only 13.69%)

### 3. **Optimize Geographic Performance**
- Top country generates **$3.8M profit** - study what's working
- **Replicate success factors:**
  - Sales team structure and strategy
  - Marketing approaches
  - Product-market fit insights
  - Customer success practices
- Apply learnings to lower-performing markets

### 4. **Segment-Specific Strategies**

**Government Segment ($53M sales):**
- High revenue, lower unit volume = premium pricing works
- Maintain relationship-driven sales approach
- Explore upsell opportunities within existing accounts

**Midmarket ($42M sales, 41.81% units):**
- High volume, moderate pricing = optimize operational efficiency
- Focus on customer retention (volume play requires consistent demand)
- Consider subscription or recurring revenue models

**Small Business & Channel Partners (underperforming):**
- Assess if current GTM strategy fits these segments
- Consider partnerships or self-service models to reduce acquisition costs
- Pilot targeted campaigns before major investment
